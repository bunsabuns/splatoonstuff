Splat Roller

A formidable close-range weapon, characterized by its wide paint roller that not only efficiently covers turf but also delivers devastating splats to opponents, making it a favored choice for inkling players who enjoy aggressive and strategic gameplay.

Carbon Roller

A swift and agile ink-flinging weapon, distinguished by its lightweight design and quick flicks, providing inklings with a nimble playstyle that excels in both turf coverage and surprising opponents with swift and precise attacks on the colorful battlefields of Inkopolis.

Dynamo Roller

A powerful but slow ink-slinging behemoth, featuring an enormous roller that covers vast amounts of turf with each swing, making it a strategic choice for players who value area control and can master the art of unleashing devastating splats with its deliberate and impactful attacks.
